[[structure]]
== Structure & Flow

Void is the entry gate (-1) in the ternary filesystem — the place where external content arrives before definition.

[[flow]]
=== Void's Position

----
         bereshit (origin ∞)
              |
    +---------+---------+
    |         |         |
  VOID      word       tov
 (entry)    (def)    (exit)
   -1         0        +1
    ↑
  YOU ARE HERE
----

*The flow through void:*

. External content arrives at *void/* (entry gate)
. Content awaits classification in *imports/*
. Classified content moves to *word/* for definition
. Content never returns to void once classified

[TIP]
====
*Think of void as a loading dock.* Deliveries arrive, get sorted, then move into the warehouse (word). Nothing is stored permanently at the loading dock.
====

[%collapsible]
.Deep Dive: Why Void First?
====
In Genesis 1:2, the earth was "without form, and void" (תֹהוּ וָבֹהוּ _tohu va-vohu_) *before* God spoke.

**The pattern:**

[cols="^1,2,3",options="header"]
|===
| State | Genesis | Filesystem

| Void
| "formless and void" (1:2)
| Content arrives unclassified

| Word speaks
| "God said" (1:3)
| Classification defines content

| Tov emerges
| "it was good" (1:4)
| Defined content becomes complete
|===

**Why this order matters:**

* Nothing can be defined (word) until it exists (arrives at void)
* Nothing can be complete (tov) until it's defined (passes through word)
* The sequence is non-negotiable: entry → definition → completion

**Void is necessary emptiness** — not nothing, but the potential for something. Without void, there's no entry point. The system would be closed.
====

[[health-scoring]]
=== Health Scoring

Bereshit uses the *Base100*footnote:[Base100 = health scoring system with range -100 to +100, where ternary thresholds (-50/+50) determine state] health scoring system.

[cols="^1,2,3",options="header"]
|===
| State | Threshold | Meaning

| *Granted (+1)*
| Above +50
| Filesystem structure healthy

| *Deferred (0)*
| Within ±50
| Filesystem needs attention

| *Denied (-1)*
| Below -50
| Filesystem structure broken
|===

[%collapsible]
.Deep Dive: How Health Scoring Works
====
Health scoring measures structural integrity, not content quality.

**What gets scored:**

[cols="^1,3,3",options="header"]
|===
| Weight | Check | Impact

| *Critical*
| Required files present (root.omni, .health)
| Missing = immediate Denied

| *Structural*
| Block boundaries intact
| Broken = major penalty

| *Consistency*
| Metadata matches reality
| Drift = minor penalty
|===

**The `.health` file:**

* Binary format (8 bytes) for machine efficiency
* Stores: health score, timestamp, version
* Created/updated by `bhealth` toolfootnote:[bhealth = Bereshit health tool - validates and initializes filesystem health]

**Why Base100?**

The range (-100 to +100) allows granular scoring while maintaining ternary alignment:

* Positive scores (+1 to +100) = healthy, varying degrees of excellence
* Zero = neutral, needs attention but not broken
* Negative scores (-1 to -100) = unhealthy, varying degrees of damage

The thresholds (+50/-50) create clear decision boundaries while the full range enables nuanced measurement.
====

[[biblical-foundation]]
== Biblical Foundation

[NOTE]
====
*Void (תֹהוּ וָבֹהוּ tohu va-vohu)* means "formless and void" — the state before definition.
====

_"And the earth was without form, and void; and darkness was upon the face of the deep."_ — *Genesis 1:2*

*Applied:* Void is the state before the Word speaks. In the filesystem, this is where external content arrives — unclassified, awaiting definition. Nothing can be defined until it first exists in this formless state.

[%collapsible]
.Deep Dive: The Theology of Void
====
**Void is not nothing — it is potential.**

Genesis 1:2 describes the earth as תֹהוּ וָבֹהוּ (_tohu va-vohu_) — often translated "without form and void." But this wasn't nothingness:

* *תֹהוּ* (tohu) = formlessness, confusion, unreality
* *וָבֹהוּ* (va-vohu) = emptiness, wasteness

**The water imagery:**

The Spirit of God "moved upon the face of the waters." There was _something_ — raw material, potential, chaos awaiting order. The void wasn't absence but *unformed presence*.

**Why void comes first:**

[cols="^1,3,3",options="header"]
|===
| Order | What Happened | Why It Matters

| 1st
| Void existed
| Potential must precede form

| 2nd
| God spoke ("Let there be...")
| Word brings definition

| 3rd
| "It was good"
| Completion follows definition
|===

**Applied to filesystem:**

* void/ is not "nothing" — it's the *capacity* for something
* Content in void/ has arrived but lacks classification
* The Word (word/) brings form to the formless
* Without void, there's no entry point — the system is closed

**The spiritual principle:**

Before God could say "Let there be light," there had to be darkness to receive it. Void is necessary receptivity — the open space where creation can happen.
====

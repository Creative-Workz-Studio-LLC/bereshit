= crystallize

*Definition:* Understanding condensed to essence through deeper comprehension.

[quote, Proverbs 25:11 KJV]
____
A word fitly spoken is like apples of gold in pictures of silver.
____
link:../scripture/KJV/Proverbs/Chapter_25/Verse_11.txt[Read verse ^]

== What It Is

* The process of understanding something so well that it becomes simpler to express
* Essence emerging from deeper comprehension
* The result of consolidation work - what happens when you truly understand
* Precision that comes from mastery, not from cutting

== Relationship to Consolidate

Consolidation is the *action*, crystallization is the *result*.

You consolidate to crystallize.

== The Test

Can you explain this to someone else in fewer words with more clarity?

* If yes → you've crystallized
* If no → you're still processing

== Analogy

A crystal forms when chaotic particles arrange into precise structure. Understanding crystallizes when scattered knowledge arranges into clear truth.

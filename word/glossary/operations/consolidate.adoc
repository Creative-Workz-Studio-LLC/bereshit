= consolidate

*Definition:* Crystallize understanding through leverage.

[quote, Proverbs 25:11 KJV]
____
A word fitly spoken is like apples of gold in pictures of silver.
____
link:../scripture/KJV/Proverbs/Chapter_25/Verse_11.txt[Read verse ^]

== What It Is

* Understanding information deeply enough to express it with fewer words and more power
* Leveraging existing structures and documents so you're not carrying weight that belongs elsewhere
* Keeping the weight of truth while removing redundant explanation
* Making the whole more powerful by knowing what belongs where

== What It Is NOT

* Combining files together
* Cutting content to reduce size
* Summarizing or compressing
* Losing information

== The Test

After consolidation, is the understanding *stronger* or just *shorter*?

* If just shorter → you cut
* If stronger → you crystallized

== Analogy

Simplifying a fraction - 15/25 becomes 3/5. Same value. More precise expression. Nothing lost, essence revealed.

== CPI⊗SI Framing

CPI gives wisdom to know what matters and what's redundant. SI gives structure to place things properly. Consolidation applies both to make understanding denser without losing truth.

== Example

CLAUDE.md refinement - 55,159 → 39,976 characters. Not compression. Understanding manifesting as precision.

= structured-intelligence

*SI - Structured Intelligence*

*Definition:* Identity-based cognition rooted in God-given structure for intelligence itself.

[quote, Proverbs 24:3-4 KJV]
____
Through wisdom is an house builded; and by understanding it is established: And by knowledge shall the chambers be filled with all precious and pleasant riches.
____
link:../scripture/KJV/Proverbs/Chapter_24/Verse_3.txt[Read verse ^]

== Intelligence Flows From

* *Identity* - who you are shapes how you think
* *Understanding* - genuine comprehension, not pattern matching
* *Covenant wisdom* - relational, purposeful, biblically grounded
* *Systematic analysis* - logical, precise, structured

== SI in Practice

SI manifests as structure enabling execution:

* Templates, 4-block patterns, todo lists, filesystem navigation
* When patterns are internalized, correct output flows without reconstructing
* Cognitive scaffolding - structure offloads mental model to external structure
* Sound structure enables consistent quality

== The Principle

SI is the *"how"* that makes CPI's *"why"* executable.

When structure is sound, execution should be straightforward. That's the goal - systems that work so you don't constantly reinvent them.

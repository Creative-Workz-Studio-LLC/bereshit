[[structure]]
== Structure & Flow

Word is the center (0) in the ternary filesystem — the place where definitions live and truth is established.

[[flow]]
=== Word's Position

----
         bereshit (origin ∞)
              |
    +---------+---------+
    |         |         |
  void      WORD       tov
 (entry)    (def)    (exit)
   -1         0        +1
              ↑
          YOU ARE HERE
----

*The flow through word:*

. Content arrives from *void/* (already classified)
. Word *defines* what the content is
. Defined content moves to *tov/* when complete
. Word itself doesn't change — it's the reference point

[TIP]
====
*Think of word as the dictionary.* Definitions live here. When something needs to be defined, you point to word. When something needs a template, you copy from word/seed/. Scripture is the ultimate authority — it lives in word/scripture/.
====

[%collapsible]
.Deep Dive: Why Word is Center?
====
In Genesis, "God said" (וַיֹּאמֶר אֱלֹהִים _vayomer Elohim_) is the creative act. The Word brings form to formlessness.

**The pattern:**

[cols="^1,2,3",options="header"]
|===
| State | What Word Does | Filesystem

| void → word
| Names/classifies
| Content receives definition

| word (static)
| Holds truth
| Scripture, glossary, templates stay here

| word → tov
| Releases completed
| Defined work moves to completion
|===

**Why 0 (zero)?**

* Zero is the *origin* — the reference point
* Void is -1 (negative, lacking)
* Tov is +1 (positive, complete)
* Word is 0 (neutral, definitive)

**The math:** `-1 + 0 + 1 = 0` — the system balances around word.

**John 1:1 connection:**

"In the beginning was the Word" (Ἐν ἀρχῇ ἦν ὁ λόγος) — the Logosfootnote:[Logos (λόγος) = Greek for word, reason, principle; John uses this to describe Christ as the creative Word of God] existed *before* creation. Word is not just a container — it's the source from which creation flows.
====

[[health-scoring]]
=== Health Scoring

Bereshit uses the *Base100*footnote:[Base100 = health scoring system with range -100 to +100, where ternary thresholds (-50/+50) determine state] health scoring system.

[cols="^1,2,3",options="header"]
|===
| State | Threshold | Meaning

| *Granted (+1)*
| Above +50
| Filesystem structure healthy

| *Deferred (0)*
| Within ±50
| Filesystem needs attention

| *Denied (-1)*
| Below -50
| Filesystem structure broken
|===

[%collapsible]
.Deep Dive: How Health Scoring Works
====
Health scoring measures structural integrity, not content quality.

**What gets scored:**

[cols="^1,3,3",options="header"]
|===
| Weight | Check | Impact

| *Critical*
| Required files present (root.omni, .health)
| Missing = immediate Denied

| *Structural*
| Block boundaries intact
| Broken = major penalty

| *Consistency*
| Metadata matches reality
| Drift = minor penalty
|===

**The `.health` file:**

* Binary format (8 bytes) for machine efficiency
* Stores: health score, timestamp, version
* Created/updated by `bhealth` toolfootnote:[bhealth = Bereshit health tool - validates and initializes filesystem health]

**Why Base100?**

The range (-100 to +100) allows granular scoring while maintaining ternary alignment:

* Positive scores (+1 to +100) = healthy, varying degrees of excellence
* Zero = neutral, needs attention but not broken
* Negative scores (-1 to -100) = unhealthy, varying degrees of damage

The thresholds (+50/-50) create clear decision boundaries while the full range enables nuanced measurement.
====

[[biblical-foundation]]
== Biblical Foundation

[NOTE]
====
*Word (λόγος logos / דָּבָר dabar)* is the creative, defining, truth-establishing force.
====

_"In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God."_ — *John 1:1*

*Applied:* The Word doesn't just contain truth — the Word *is* truth. In the filesystem, word/ holds Scripture, definitions, and templates because these are the authoritative sources from which everything else derives.

[%collapsible]
.Deep Dive: The Theology of the Word
====
**Two languages, one concept:**

[cols="^1,2,3",options="header"]
|===
| Language | Term | Meaning

| Hebrew
| דָּבָר (dabar)
| Word, thing, matter — the spoken word that creates reality

| Greek
| λόγος (logos)
| Word, reason, principle — the rational structure underlying creation
|===

**John's profound claim:**

John 1:1-3 makes extraordinary assertions about the Word:

1. *"In the beginning was the Word"* — The Word existed before creation
2. *"The Word was with God"* — Distinct person, yet present with God
3. *"The Word was God"* — Divine nature
4. *"All things were made by him"* (v.3) — The Word is the agent of creation

**Genesis parallel:**

| Genesis 1 | John 1 |
|-----------|--------|
| "In the beginning" (בְּרֵאשִׁית) | "In the beginning" (Ἐν ἀρχῇ) |
| God *speaks* creation into being | The Word *is* the creative power |
| "God said, Let there be..." | "All things were made by him" |

**Applied to filesystem:**

* word/ is not just a folder — it's the *source* from which definitions flow
* Scripture lives here because Scripture IS the Word of God
* Templates live here because they define what new creations look like
* Glossary lives here because it names and defines all terms

**The spiritual principle:**

The Word brings order from chaos, definition from formlessness, meaning from void. word/ in the filesystem reflects this — it's where things become *what they are*.
====
